CONCLUSION

In this Mini-Project, PowerBI's data visualization and analytics capabilities provided crucial insights into the COVID-19 pandemic. Analysis of diverse datasets, including infection and vaccination rates, offered a comprehensive view of the pandemic's peak. Our interactive dashboard enabled users to explore data, emphasizing data-driven decision-making during crises.
